About

I like playing Fortnite more than other games because it is a Battle Royale game with a variety of items and weapons and loads of possibilities. It has a gripping storyline too! It is a never ending game so you can keep playing on and on. They update regularly to fix bugs and update the storyline. You can use real life money to get game currency to buy different outfits, backpacks, dances and many more!
